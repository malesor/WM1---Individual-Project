About Us

GERMIA GYM was founded by five great athletes who wanted to give back to the city of Prishtina. Founded in 2017, it includes the best facilities to accomodate different age groups. You can choose amongst the sports that we offer, such as: Football and Kickboxing. Through our platform you can book your preferred sports arena as well as your desired date and time.

Our Location

We are only as good as the place we live in, that's why choose to build our amazing facility in the outskirts of Prishtina. Here at our GYM, you will get the opportunity to enjoy the beautiful nature of Germia and escape from your everyday life. The view is beautiful during the day and even more hypnotizing at night. Don't miss out on some fresh air and lots of fun!
